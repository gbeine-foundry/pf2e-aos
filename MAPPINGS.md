# PF2E – Aventurien on Steroids: Rule Elements Overview

All rule elements introduced by AoS are tagged with the "Aventurien" trait.

## Mapping Overview 

As this is a migration guide from the DSA/TDE rules to Pathfinder, the structure follows the first.

### Core Rule Elements

DSA Eigenschaften are mapped to the Pathfinder Abilities.
While DSA uses 8 Eigenschaften, Pathfinder uses 6 Abilities.

## Mapping Details

### Half-Elf

* The PF2 rules consider half-elfes to be a [human heritage](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?HalfHuman=true), while DSA rules treat half-elfes as a [separate species](https://ulisses-regelwiki.de/Spez_Halbelfen.html)
* AoS takes a compromise and introduces the species of half-elfes which is a copy of the [human ancestry](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?ID=6) from the core rules with the additional traits gained from the [half-elf heritage](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?ID=7).
* Aventurian half-elfes can only select from a limited number of cultures, these are
  * Auelf (Glade Elf), granted by selecting Auelf as heritage
  * Firnelf (Snow Elf), granted by selecting Firnelf as heritage
  * Waldelf (Wood Elf), granted by selecting Woodelf as heritage
  * Andergaster (Andergastan, Kingdom of Andergast), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Bornländer (Bornlander, Region of Born River), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Horasier (Horasian Empire), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Mittelreicher (Middenrealmer), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Nivesen (Nivese), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Nordaventurier (Northern Aventurian), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Nostrier (Kingdom of Nostria), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Svelttaler (Svellter, Region of Svelt River), option when selecting a human heritage
  * Thorwaler (Thorwaler, Region of Thorwal), option when selecting a human heritage
* Selecting the AoS half-elf ancestry enforces the selection of a compatible heritage and background (both can be changed technically afterwards, but that would not make any sense).

### Dwarf

* The PF2 rules consider the [dwarf ancestry](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?ID=1) to have the [clan dagger](https://2e.aonprd.com/Weapons.aspx?ID=13) which is a cultural artefact that seems to be incompatible with the aventurian interpretation of dwarfes.
* AoS takes a compromise and introduces the a copy of the [dwarf ancestry](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?ID=1) from the core rules without the clan dagger.
* Dwarfes in Aventuria are a quite robust species and start with a higher number of hit points than other PC species, therefore the AoS darf ancestry grant additional hit points of half the level of the character.
* Selecting the AoS dwarf ancestry enforces the selection of a compatible heritage and background (both can be changed technically afterwards, but that would not make any sense).

### Human Heritages

* DSA defines 7 different types of humans each with limited access to different cultures, there is no similar concept in the PF2 rules.
* AoS implements these 7 different types as heritages for the core [human ancestry](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?ID=6).
* Selecting one of the AoS human heritages enforces the selection of a compatible culture as background (this can be changed technically afterwards, but that would not make any sense).

### Auelfen

* Auelf is a culture in DSA and splitted into a heritage and a background for PF2.
* The heritage requires the ancestry [Elf](https://2e.aonprd.com/Ancestries.aspx?ID=2) or the above mentioned ancestry half-elf.
* The heritage grants an AoS specific feat Elfen Ancestry that grants the following feats
  * [Elven Lore](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=13) on level 1, which leads to be trained in arcana and nature as well as access to [Elven Lore](https://2e.aonprd.com/Skills.aspx?ID=8).
  * [Elven Verve](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=976) on level 1, as long as the character is not half-elf
  * [Elven Weapon Familiarity](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=14) on level 1
  * [Elven Weapon Elegance](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=20) on level 5
  * [Elven Weapon Expertise](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=24) on level 13
* The background provides no trained skill and no trained lore as both are granted through the heritage with the [Elven Lore](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=13) feat.
